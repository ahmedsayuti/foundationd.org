WHO WE ARE

We empower communities, families, and individuals.

The Foundation for a Healthy North Dakota promotes health and wellness by empowering communities, families, and individuals to build a healthier North Dakota. Founded in 2022, Foundation for a Healthy North Dakota proudly serves the 53 counties of North Dakota.
